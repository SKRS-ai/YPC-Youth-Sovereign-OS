The Social Contract

Article I: Institutional Integrity

The Commitment to Service

Every Young Politician admitted to this Collective agrees that political leadership is a form of sacred service. You agree to use the tools of the **Civic Lab** and the **Gavel Initiative** solely for the advancement of your community and the protection of the Village.

Article II: The Goodness Code

Ethical Development

You agree to adhere to the **Goodness Code** in all digital and physical interactions. Harassment, misinformation, and the pursuit of power for personal wealth are grounds for immediate credential revocation from the **Sovereign Registry**.

Article III: Data Guardianship

Ownership of Legacy

While the YPC provides the infrastructure, you and your Legal Guardian remain the owners of your intellectual property and policy drafts. You grant the YPC a non-exclusive license to display your works within the **Foundry Marketplace** for the benefit of the collective.
