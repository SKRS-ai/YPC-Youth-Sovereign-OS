The Privacy Shield

VERIFIED COPPA COMPLIANT // 2026 STATUS

I. Data Sovereignty for Minor Candidates

The YPC recognizes that every Young Politician is the sole owner of their developmental data. Unlike traditional educational platforms, we do not track for advertisement or metadata sale. All records within the Developmental Ledger are encrypted with a private key held by the Legal Guardian.

II. The "Mind Damage" Protection Protocol

Consistent with our mission to prevent Mind Damage, our digital infrastructure includes "Safe-Interactions." Communication between mentors and youth is logged and accessible to Guardians at all times to ensure the highest ethical standards of the Goodness Code.

III. Biometric & Identity Security

When using the Physical Pillar ID, biometric hashes are stored locally on the card's NFC chip. No biometric data is stored on our central servers, ensuring that a youth's identity remains private and unhackable.
